HR Attrition Analysis.

Project Overview:
Analyzed IBM HR dataset of 1,470 employee records 
to identify key drivers of employee attrition 
using Python and MySQL.

Tools Used:
Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
MySQL (pymysql)
Jupyter Notebook

Key Findings:
1. 16% overall attrition rate
2. Sales department has highest attrition
3. Employees who left earned 30% less salary
4. 25-35 age group leaves the most
5. Employees under 2 years experience leave most

Business Recommendation:
Companies should focus salary revision and retention programs for young Sales employees in their first 2 years.
